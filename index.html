About Me

Born on July 19, 1995, in Oyo, Nigeria, and raised in Malaysia, I am Elijah Snoz, a professional artist, founder of West Group Ent, and CEO of X Wurld, a software company. I studied Computer Science at Linton University College and Software Engineering with ALX Africa in Nigeria. Currently, I am working on my debut album release in Nigeria.
